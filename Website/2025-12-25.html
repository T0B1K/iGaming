Expanse Studios Secures Swedish iGaming License

Golden Matrix subsidiary expands regulated footprint in Europe

November 1, 2015 Jane Doe

Expanse Studios has officially received its B2B gaming license from the Swedish Gambling Authority, opening doors to supply its portfolio of proprietary games to regulated operators across Sweden. This milestone represents a key step in the Golden Matrix Group’s European expansion strategy, allowing the company to strengthen its presence in one of the continent’s most mature iGaming markets. Operators now gain access to high-quality, compliant content, supporting the growing demand for licensed digital gaming solutions.

The licensing approval comes after months of preparation and regulatory review, highlighting the company’s commitment to transparency and adherence to local standards. By entering the Swedish market, Expanse Studios not only increases its footprint but also sets a benchmark for other B2B providers seeking opportunities in tightly regulated jurisdictions. Analysts suggest this move could accelerate partnerships with leading operators and boost the company’s influence across Northern Europe.

Expanse Studios plans to roll out its titles gradually, ensuring seamless integration and performance for operators and players alike. The company emphasizes a focus on innovation and user engagement, promising a suite of games designed to enhance player experiences while complying with all regulatory requirements. This strategic expansion reinforces Golden Matrix’s reputation for reliability and positions the brand for sustained growth throughout 2026 and beyond.
